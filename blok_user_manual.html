Blok User Role

Your Role as Blok User

As a Blok User, you are responsible for managing peace keepers within your specific Blok area. You have access to register peace keepers, record crime cases, manage attendance, and request identity cards.

Your Blok: You can only view and manage data within your assigned Blok. System automatically filters all data based on your Blok.

Quick Actions

Register Peace Keeper

Add new peace keepers to your Blok with personal info, photo, and contact details.

Register Crime Case

Report crime cases in your Blok with location, description, and status.

QR Attendance

Take attendance using QR code scanner or manual entry.

Request ID Card

Request identity cards for peace keepers in your Blok.

1. Register Peace Keeper

  • 1 Go to Peace Keepers → Register Peace Keeper
  • 2 Fill in personal information (Full Name, Gender, Age, Date of Birth)
  • 3 Fill in address (Ketena, Blok, House No)
  • 4 Fill in contact information (Phone, Emergency Contact)
  • 5 Upload a photo (required)
  • 6 Click Register Peace Keeper button
Tip: All fields marked with * are required. Your Blok will be auto-filled from your account.

2. Register Crime Case

  • 1 Go to Crime Cases → Register Crime
  • 2 Enter case name and description
  • 3 Select date and time (Ethiopian calendar)
  • 4 Enter location (Ketena, Blok)
  • 5 Select response action and status
  • 6 Click Submit Crime Case button
Tip: Use the map to mark exact location of the crime.

3. QR Attendance Management

  • 1 Go to Attendance → Insert Attendance
  • 2 Select Check In or Check Out tab
  • 3 Choose entry method: QR Scanner or Manual Entry
  • 4 For QR: Click "Start Scanner" and scan member's QR code
  • 5 For Manual: Enter Member ID and click "Search"
  • 6 Click Check In to record attendance
Important: Each member can only check in ONCE per day. Duplicate check-ins are automatically rejected.

4. Bulk Attendance Insertion

  • 1 Go to Attendance → Bulk Attendance Insertion
  • 2 Select Day and Shift from dropdown
  • 3 View scheduled peace keepers
  • 4 Select members to check in (checkbox)
  • 5 Click Register Selected button
Tip: Bulk attendance saves time when registering multiple members at once.

5. View Data

View Peace Keepers

See all peace keepers in your Blok. Use filters and search to find specific members.

View Crime Cases

View crime cases in your Blok with status and details.

Attendance Data

View attendance records with check-in/out times and duration.

6. Request Identity Card

  • 1 Go to Identity Cards → Request Identity Card
  • 2 Select peace keepers who need ID cards
  • 3 Click Request Selected button
  • 4 Request sent to District Admin for approval
Tip: Once approved, you can print the identity card from the management page.

7. Using Filters & Search

  • 1 Use cascading filters: Sub City → District → Ketena → Blok
  • 2 Use search bar to find by name, ID, or phone
  • 3 Click Apply button to apply filters
  • 4 Click Reset to clear all filters
Tip: Tables support pagination - use Previous/Next buttons to navigate through records.

8. Keyboard Shortcuts

Submit forms / Search

Esc

Ctrl/Cmd + P

Print current page
